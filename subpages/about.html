ABOUT US

The Pre-PA club at Santa Clara University was established to bring together students that are interested in becoming Physician Assistants, and educated students and the community about the Physician Assistant profession as well as provide opportunites that prepare students for entry to PA school.

Fun Fact: We were established in Fall 2020!

What can we offer you?

  • Guest Speakers (PA's, admissions counselors from PA schools, current PA students, medical professionals, program directors from regional PA programs)
  • Eudcational Information on the history and purpose of the profession, along with admissions requirements, application procedures, and health experience opportunities
  • Workshops regarding pre-requistite classes and the CASPA application
  • Workshops regarding GRE Prep Courses
  • Interview and Personal Statement tips & practices
  • PA Mentoring Program
  • Field trip to regional PA Programs
  • Set up shadowing programs/relationships
  • Local philantrophic efforts (PA/Healthcare related), and more!
